Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 18, 2025

Overview

Spokable PDF Converter is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use our service.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data.

Our application runs entirely in your web browser (client-side). We have no backend servers, databases, or data collection mechanisms.

What Happens to Your Files

  • PDF files you upload are processed entirely in your browser
  • Files are never uploaded to our servers (we don't have any)
  • Extracted text is sent directly from your browser to Google's Gemini API
  • We never see, access, or store your documents
  • All processing happens on your device

API Keys

  • Your Google AI Studio API key is stored locally in your browser's localStorage
  • The API key never leaves your device excep making direct API calls to Google
  • We cannot access your API key
  • You can clear your API key at any time by clearing your browser data

Third-Party Services

When you use our service, your browser communicates directly with:

  • Google Gemini API: Text is sent to Google's servers for AI processing. Review Google's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle data.

Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to save:

  • Your API key (optional, for convenience)
  • Your settings and preferences
  • Custom prompt templates

This data is stored locally on your device and can be cleared at any time through your browser settings.

Analytics

We do not use any analytics, tracking, or monitoring tools. We have no way to track your usage of this application.

Data Security

Since all processing happens in your browser:

  • Your data security depends on your device and browser security
  • Use HTTPS to access our site (enforced automatically)
  • Keep your browser and operating system updated
  • Use strong passwords and secure your API keys

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us.
